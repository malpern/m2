5 Quick Questions

Before we wire up the texting, we need to nail down a few things. Should take 2 minutes.

1. What days and times are you available?
List your training slots for each day. Skip days you don't work.
2. Do any clients have a standing slot every week?
People who just show up without you texting them. We'll lock those in automatically.
3. When you text clients, do you send a few at once or one at a time?
And when do you start — Sunday night, Monday morning?
4. How long do you wait before following up or moving on?
If someone doesn't reply, how long before you text again or give their slot to someone else?
5. What do your texts usually sound like?
Paste or type a couple real examples so the AI can match your voice.
