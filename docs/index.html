What You'll See

Six screens. Everything you need to run your training business, in one place. Open it in any browser on your phone, laptop, or tablet.

Dashboard

Your home base. See this week at a glance: how many sessions are booked, who hasn't replied yet, which clients are running low on their packages, and anything that needs your attention.

Schedule

Your weekly calendar — the Tetris board. Hit "Generate Week" and the system fills in time slots using your rules. Drag and drop to make adjustments. When it looks right, approve it and the outreach texts go out.

Clients

Your full roster. See everyone ranked by priority. Add new clients, change someone's status (active, on break, in-season), adjust their behavior score, set preferred days and times. The priority ranking updates automatically.

Outreach

Packages

Track every client's package balance. See sessions purchased vs. sessions delivered. Get alerts when someone is running low, or when you've front-loaded sessions that haven't been paid for yet.

Reports

Weekly and monthly summaries. Sessions completed, revenue tracked, client retention trends. Everything you currently log by hand on Sundays, done automatically.

Your Priority System

We built this from exactly how you described it. College-bound kids come first, then grade level, then who's actually putting in the work. This is what the system uses to decide who gets texted first and who gets the best time slots.

How the ranking works

1 College-bound athletes always come first. A college-bound sophomore outranks a non-college senior.
2 Within that, grade level matters. Seniors before juniors, juniors before sophomores, sophomores before freshmen.
3 Behavior score breaks ties. The kid who shows up every week and works hard ranks higher than the kid who talks big but doesn't follow through.
4 3pm slots get filled first because they're the hardest to fill. Then 5pm, then 6pm.

Your Scheduling Rules

These are the rules the system follows when it builds your week. They come straight from how you described your process.

1 Fill 3pm slots first — they're the hardest to fill and the most valuable to lock down early
2 Then fill 5pm before 6pm — if 5pm fills up, you might not need to stay until 7
3 If 5pm doesn't fill, move those athletes to 6pm instead
4 Every serious athlete gets at least 1 session per week
5 Only your top-tier college-bound athletes get 2 sessions per week
6 No Saturdays unless absolutely necessary — that's family time. Sundays are full workdays
7 Extra sessions only go to lower-priority athletes if there's leftover space in the week
