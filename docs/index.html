What you do vs. what the system does

You (required)

  • Confirm your availability each week
  • Review and approve the proposed schedule
  • Hit "Send Outreach" to start texting
  • Decide on reschedule requests and unclear replies

System (automatic)

  • Fills standing slots automatically
  • Generates schedule using your priority rules
  • Sends texts via WhatsApp through Twilio
  • Reads and classifies every reply with AI
  • Follows up after 1 hour, moves on after 3
  • Reads your Google Calendar to avoid conflicts
  • Tracks package balances and flags missed deductions

You (optional)

  • Drag-and-drop to rearrange the schedule before sending
  • Override any AI classification
  • Tune the priority algorithm — adjust how much college, grade, and effort matter
  • Pin or unpin clients in the ranking
  • Edit session times right from the dashboard
  • Export .csv to Google Sheets
  • Review reports and package reconciliation
